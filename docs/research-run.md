# Research Run: Library Growth Scan

Run cadence: twice weekly by default, plus ad hoc when major signals arrive.

## Input classes

- Hypernovelty Daily Digest signals
- AI/workflow tools
- education/learning signals
- legal/healthcare/institutional trust signals
- media-trust/synthetic consensus examples
- user-shared links and private notes
- repo/tool launches relevant to adaptation

## Scoring questions

For each candidate signal/tool:

- What new adaptation burden does this reveal?
- Who is carrying the burden?
- What changed faster than the institution/workflow could update?
- What verification layer is missing?
- What decision would a person/team need to make differently?
- Could a small free primitive help?
- Would a paid version be justified by customization, hosting, or expert review?

## Output format

Each run should produce a local review packet with:

- 5–12 candidate ideas
- recommended lane: repo/free, paid later, HN article, iPublishOS, Future of Education, Human Premium, monitor, or park
- one suggested artifact name
- smallest proof artifact
- genericity-test risk
- safety/claim caveats

No public publishing or account actions occur without explicit approval.
