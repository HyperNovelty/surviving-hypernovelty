**Review owner:** Audience-trust editor  
**Review date:** 2026-06-29  
**Confidence after review:** medium

## Original item / first trusted copy
- Description: Synthetic storm-shelter photo supplied directly by a staff photographer for media-literacy practice.
- Source or holder: Newsroom asset folder; synthetic file only; no real person or event depicted.
- Credential check: Credential inspected in the newsroom asset tool before export; signer and timestamp recorded in local notes, not reproduced here.
- Credential result: present_and_checked

## Alternate corroboration
- Compare the classroom screenshot against the publisher page and original synthetic asset note.
- Check whether the caption, date, and location were preserved across each reuse.
- Ask a human editor or teacher to decide whether the slide needs a separate context note before use.

## Do not claim from this receipt
- Do not claim the credential proves the depicted event happened as captioned.
- Do not claim the screenshot is fake just because credentials were stripped.
- Do not use this synthetic example as evidence about real storms, shelters, platforms, or credential tool performance.
