## Gate before building paid versions

A paid version should pass all of these:

- It is not equivalent to a one-shot AI prompt.
- It needs customization, review, hosting, integration, or institutional context.
- It creates a repeatable artifact people can actually use.
- It has a clear buyer/user and painful current job.
- It preserves trust boundaries and avoids fake authority.
