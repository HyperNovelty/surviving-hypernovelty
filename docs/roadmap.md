## Recurring growth loop

A regular research run should inspect incoming signals/tools and ask:

1. Does this reveal a new adaptation burden?
2. Does it expose a trust boundary, verification gap, or learning/workflow gap?
3. Could a small free primitive help?
4. Is there a paid version only if customization/hosting/review adds real value?
5. Should it be routed to Hypernovelty, iPublishOS, Future of Education, Human Premium, or parked?

Outputs should be review-only idea cards, not automatic publishing or product claims.
