# Surviving Hypernovelty Kit Index

Choose by situation, not by primitive name.

1. [Agent Delegation Safety Kit](kits/agent-delegation-safety-kit.md) — Before an AI agent touches tools, files, customers, accounts, money, publishing, or internal systems, make its authority reviewable.
2. [Scam / Deepfake Verification Kit](kits/scam-deepfake-verification-kit.md) — Slow down high-pressure impersonation, fraud, and bad source-chain decisions before people click, pay, share codes, or approve sensitive actions.
3. [AI Work Quality Kit](kits/ai-work-quality-kit.md) — Separate generated/drafted work from reviewed, owned, repairable work.
4. [Future of Education Evidence Kit](kits/future-of-education-evidence-kit.md) — Move from AI-cheating panic to visible learning evidence, local validity, and clear disclosure rules.
5. [Source Trust / Answer-Layer Kit](kits/source-trust-answer-layer-kit.md) — Make page-level claims more reviewable and citation-ready without promising search visibility or laundering provenance into truth.
