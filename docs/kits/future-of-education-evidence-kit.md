# Future of Education Evidence Kit

**Public promise:** Move from AI-cheating panic to visible learning evidence, local validity, and clear disclosure rules.

**Audience:** Teachers, students, program chairs, instructional designers, academic-integrity leads.

## Use this when

- A person or team has a concrete decision to make under fast-changing AI/information conditions.
- The output needs to be reviewable without uploading private data.
- A synthetic example is enough to teach the workflow before customization.

## Included primitives

- Learning Dossier Folder Template
- Assessment Evidence Packet Lite
- Assessment Goal Drift Repair Card
- Visible Thinking Repair Ticket
- Shadow AI Amnesty Triage Sheet

## Candidate extension handling

Shadow AI Habit-to-Curriculum Translation Sheet is now an education example attached to Shadow AI Amnesty, not a new standalone schema.

## Boundary

Does not normalize cheating, data leakage, AI-detector certainty, or automated grading.

## Open-source release notes

- Keep examples synthetic.
- Keep browser-local/offline paths prominent.
- Do not imply legal, security, academic-integrity, medical, financial, or compliance authority.
- Paid value later must come from customization, facilitation, hosting, recurring review, or implementation support — not from guaranteed outcomes.
