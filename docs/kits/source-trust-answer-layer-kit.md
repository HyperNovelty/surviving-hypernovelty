# Source Trust / Answer-Layer Kit

**Public promise:** Make page-level claims more reviewable and citation-ready without promising search visibility or laundering provenance into truth.

**Audience:** Publishers, researchers, nonprofits, institutional comms, creators, iPublishOS users.

## Use this when

- A person or team has a concrete decision to make under fast-changing AI/information conditions.
- The output needs to be reviewable without uploading private data.
- A synthetic example is enough to teach the workflow before customization.

## Included primitives

- Source Confidence Ledger
- Answer-Layer Citation Readiness Card
- Provenance Interpretation Label Card
- Provenance Breakage Receipt
- Platformized News Diet Receipt

## Candidate extension handling

Answer-Engine Source Fitness Receipt should be an example/variant under Answer-Layer Citation Readiness before becoming standalone.

## Boundary

No GEO-spam framing; does not guarantee AI citation, ranking, traffic, trust, or correctness.

## Open-source release notes

- Keep examples synthetic.
- Keep browser-local/offline paths prominent.
- Do not imply legal, security, academic-integrity, medical, financial, or compliance authority.
- Paid value later must come from customization, facilitation, hosting, recurring review, or implementation support — not from guaranteed outcomes.
