## v0.1.0-public-candidate

- Established the early public-candidate shape of Surviving Hypernovelty as a local-first starter kit.
- Froze the v0.1 primitive count at 30 existing primitives rather than adding primitive #31.
- Grouped the existing primitives into five public-facing kits:
  - Agent Delegation Safety Kit
  - Scam / Deepfake Verification Kit
  - AI Work Quality Kit
  - Future of Education Evidence Kit
  - Source Trust / Answer-Layer Kit
- Added public triage guidance for routing new ideas to examples, kit pages, articles, parking, or future primitive review.
- Kept all examples synthetic and all tools local-first; outputs remain review aids, not certification or high-stakes advice.

This entry describes a candidate state in the repository. It is not a statement
